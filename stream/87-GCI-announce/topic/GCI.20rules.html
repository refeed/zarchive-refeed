Alicja Raszkowska (Dec 25 2016 at 13:36):

You should thus claim a task before you start working on it and remember you can only have one task claimed at a time.

We've been encouraging you to find other ways of spending your time productively - helping other students, reading articles from the reading list and learning. The number of tasks is not the only factor we'll take into account. Your engagement, quality of your work and value for the community will be equally important.

Please do adhere to the GCI rules - we can't change them and need to enforce them to make sure all the students are treated with fairness they deserve.
