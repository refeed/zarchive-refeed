Steve Howell (showell) (Jan 30 2017 at 17:43):

Just to highlight some of the positive things GCI students did for Zulip:

  • We increased our integration coverage.
  • A couple students fixed some pretty subtle bugs.
  • Our contrib_bots project got battle-tested, and we have lots more bots, better documentation, better organization, and more vision for how to proceed in the future.
  • Students made some nice code cleanups with eslint, pep8, etc.
  • We have students who continue to contribute to our community and help newcomers to the project.
  • Our new online user help system is amazing!

I am probably leaving stuff out.
